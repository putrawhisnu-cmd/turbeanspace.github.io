Our Story

More than a café — it's your space

Turbean Space sits on Jl. Dr. Soetomo No.33, Gresik — a welcoming spot that balances modern design with a genuinely relaxed atmosphere. Whether you're settling in with a laptop, catching up with friends over croissants, or just here for a quiet moment with a good cup of coffee, this place was made for you.

With indoor seating, a rooftop terrace, free parking, and a playlist that just feels right — Turbean Space is Gresik's best-kept secret. But not for long.
